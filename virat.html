VIRAT KHOLI

ACHIEVEMENTS:

  • Virat Kohli is the only Indian batsman who got 890 rating points in the ICC ODI ranking.
  • Virat Kohli becomes the first Indian cricketer to cross 10,000 T20 runs.
  • Virat won the T20I series in South Africa, England, New Zealand, and Australia.
  • Virat Kohli becomes the first Indian captain in history to win two Boxing Day Test matches.
  • Virat Kohli is the third cricketer in the world after Rickey Ponting and Don Bradman, who scored three double hundreds in the calendar year.
