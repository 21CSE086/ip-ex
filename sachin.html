SACHIN TENDULKAR

ACHIEVEMENTS:

  • The first person to score a double century.
  • Tendulkar is the only player in the history of cricket to have scored 100 international centuries and has many other records under his belt which look unbreakable. Thanks to his remarkable career, Tendulkar is the ‘God of Cricket’ to almost every Indian cricket fan..
  • In his illustrious career that lasted for 24 years – from November 15, 1989, to November 16, 2013 – Tendulkar rewrote the history books, breaking several records.
  • Tendulkar played 200 Test matches for India, the most by anyone in the history of the game. But it might come as a surprise that he did not face the most number of deliveries in Tests..
  • Sachin Tendulkar featured in six editions of 50-over World Cups and it was in his final appearance in the marquee event that he finally managed to get his hands on the silverware. .
  • 1999: Padma Shri - India's civilian medal of recognition 2008: Padma Vibhushan, India's second highest civilian award.
